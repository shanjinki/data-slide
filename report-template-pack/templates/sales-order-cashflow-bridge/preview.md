# Sales Order Cashflow Bridge

Use for CRM/ERP sales order exports where the business question is whether orders are approved, fulfilled, invoiced, collected, and still carrying receivable risk.

Best for:

- ERP sales order lists
- CRM order-to-cash reviews
- shipment / delivery status exports
- invoice and collection tracking
- receivables follow-up meetings

Core modules:

1. Order, line, sales amount, collection rate, shipment completion, invoice completion KPIs
2. Approval -> execution -> shipment -> invoice -> collection -> close bridge
3. Customer sales contribution and receivables ranking
4. Shipment, invoice, and collection status distributions
5. Product/material contribution from item lines
6. Exception register for unapproved, unshipped, uninvoiced, unpaid, negative receivable, or mismatched records
7. Action plan split across sales, warehouse, finance, and management follow-up

Avoid:

- Consumer ecommerce order reports where GMV, channel, refund, campaign, or platform growth is the main story.
